Troubleshooting

No dialogues detected?

Solution: Make sure your script has clear dialogue indicators like "says", "replies", or use quotation marks around spoken text.

Too many scenes?

Solution: Your script has over 38 dialogue scenes. Consider splitting it into multiple parts or removing some dialogue.

Generation taking too long?

Solution: Wait patiently (up to 30 seconds). If it fails, try refreshing and pasting a shorter script.

Dialogue cut off?

Solution: The tool automatically splits long dialogues. Check if the scene was divided into multiple parts.
